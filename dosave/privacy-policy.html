Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2025

Welcome to Dosave, a task management and draft-saving app developed by BESAOCT. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app. By using Dosave, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect personal identifiable information (e.g., name, email) unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., for feedback).
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage data, such as app interactions and crash reports, to enhance performance and fix bugs.
  • Content Data: Tasks, drafts, and notes you create in Dosave are stored locally on your device using secure storage (Hive).
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain Dosave’s functionality, such as saving tasks and drafts.
  • Improve the app through anonymized analytics and crash reports.
  • Respond to user inquiries or feedback if you contact us.
  • Comply with legal obligations, if applicable.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your content (tasks, drafts, notes) is stored locally on your device using Hive, a secure local database. We do not transmit your content to our servers unless explicitly required (e.g., future cloud sync with your consent). We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Please back up important data regularly.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) for anonymized usage data.
  • To comply with legal requirements or protect our rights.
  • With your consent, if applicable (e.g., for cloud sync features in future updates).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or delete your data stored in Dosave by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling data collection in the app’s Settings screen (if available).
  • Contact us at xhafin@gmail.com for questions or requests about your data.
